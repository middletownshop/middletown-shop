## Video Player

Each tutorial card should contain a modern HTML5 video player.

Do NOT use YouTube.

Do NOT use any third-party embedded player.

Use the browser's built-in HTML5 video player.

Example:

```tsx
<video
  controls
  preload="metadata"
  className="w-full rounded-xl"
>
  <source src="/videos/how-to-buy-products.mp4" type="video/mp4" />
  Your browser does not support video playback.
</video>
```

For now, use placeholder video paths inside:

```text
public/videos/
```

Example files:

